Frozen Cheese Salad

Salad of the Future

Vintage recipe that will guarantee that you will see results in 30 days after implementing this amazing weight loss recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons chives
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
  • 1 teaspoon barbecue spice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups raw broccoli florets
  • Green pepper strips, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Let cheeses stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Place in blender container with buttermilk, chives, lemon peel, barbecue spice, and Worcester-shire, process at medium speed until mixture is smooth.
  3. Transfer to freezer tray.
  4. Freeze at least 3 hours.
  5. Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serv-ing. Unmold on serving platter.
  6. Surround with broc-coli florets. Garnish with green pepper strips.
  7. Divide evenly.
  8. Makes 8 luncheon servings.
